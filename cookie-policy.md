## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and remember your preferences.

## Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|------|---------|----------|
| Session cookie | Essential | Maintains your login session | Expires on logout or after inactivity |
| OAuth state | Essential | Prevents cross-site request forgery during login | 10 minutes |

## What We Do NOT Use

- No analytics or tracking cookies
- No third-party advertising cookies
- No social media tracking pixels
- No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

## Essential Cookies Only

We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for the site to function. These cookies:
- Cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality
- Do not track you across other websites
- Do not collect personal information beyond your authenticated session
- Are not shared with third parties

## Third-Party Cookies

Our payment processor (Stripe) may set cookies during the checkout process. These are governed by [Stripe's Cookie Policy](https://stripe.com/cookies-policy/legal).

## Your Rights

Since we only use essential cookies, no consent banner is required under most regulations. However, you can always clear cookies through your browser settings.
