## Introduction

Mathetes LLC ("Mathetes," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website mathetes.ai and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information:** name, email address, password (hashed)
- **Profile data:** learning preferences, course selections
- **Payment information:** processed by Stripe — we do not store credit card numbers
- **Customer support:** any information you provide when contacting us

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Log data:** IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
- **Learning activity:** course progress, quiz results, completion status
- **Cookies:** session cookies for authentication (no third-party tracking cookies)

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and personalize the learning experience
- To track course progress and issue completions
- To process payments and manage subscriptions
- To send transactional emails (account verification, course updates)
- To respond to customer support inquiries
- To monitor and maintain site security and performance

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics
- We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not collect information from children under 18

## AI Features

Mathetes uses AI to enhance the learning experience. The following applies to all AI features:

- AI conversations are **never stored server-side** — browser session only
- AI interactions are clearly labeled: "You are interacting with an AI assistant, not a human."
- AI-generated content is for educational purposes and may not always be accurate
- No AI interaction data is used to train models or shared with third parties
- Compliant with California SB 243

## Data Storage and Security

- Account and learning data is stored in our database (Cloudflare D1)
- Static assets are stored in Cloudflare R2
- Session data is stored in Cloudflare KV and expires automatically
- Payment processing is handled by Stripe — we do not store credit card numbers
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS
