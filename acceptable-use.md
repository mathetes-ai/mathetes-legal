**Mathetes LLC**

## Purpose

This policy outlines acceptable use of the Mathetes platform and services.

## Prohibited Activities

You may not:

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to any part of the site or services
- Use the services to transmit malware, spam, or malicious content
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any part of the platform
- Use the services for any illegal purpose
- Impersonate another person or entity
- Interfere with or disrupt the services or infrastructure
- Scrape, crawl, or use automated tools to extract data or content
- Share login credentials with unauthorized parties
- Use AI features to generate harmful, misleading, or illegal content
- Attempt to manipulate or exploit AI features to bypass safety measures
- Redistribute or resell course content or AI-generated outputs

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Suspension or termination of access
- Notification to appropriate authorities if illegal activity is suspected

## Reporting

Report violations to support@mathetes.ai.
